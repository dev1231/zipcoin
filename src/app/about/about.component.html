Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use ZipCoin to send money?

Sending money with ZipCoin is easy, secure and fast! We give you choices, so you can decide how you want to send or receive money. First, use your email address to create a free account on our website or through our app in the App Store or Google Play. Enter the amount of money you want to send, your beneficiary’s information, and your payment information. Depending on where your beneficiary is located, he/she may have multiple options for how to receive money including bank transfer, cash pick up, or home delivery. Click here to see our partners and options for how to receive money. Your transfer is important to us and you as our number one customer, so we provide status updates regularly that allow you to easily track your transfer. When you first submit your transfer, you’ll receive a receipt with your reference number. We will send you an update if you make any changes to the transfer, or if it is delayed for any reason. After the transfer completes, both you and the beneficiary will receive a notification that the money has arrived. At any time, you can sign into your account and select “Transactions” to view all of your status updates. ZipCoin works with thousands of trusted partners worldwide to deliver money safely, securely, and on time. ZipCoin is the first company worldwide to offer customers a standard low flat rate fee of $8.99 CAD, $6.99 USD, 5.99 EURO, 4.99 POUND for $101-9.999; 4.99 CAD, 3.99 USD, 3.99 EURO, 2.99 POUND for $50-100 and the money arrives in minutes.
